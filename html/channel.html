All rights recived

The following matters shall be put up to the Minister
1. All matters of policy
2. All matters having policy implication
3. Any scheme / guideline / standing orders / Draft Regulations
4. Cabinet Notes / PIB Notes / Memoranda to Commissions / Policy guidelines to States etc
5. Any matter having any inter-ministerial implications
6. Comments on Cabinet Note of other Ministries / Response to the communications from other Ministries / State Governments
7. Progress Reports of Schemes / Performance Reviews of Boards / Corporations under the Ministry
8. Parliamentary Matters
9. Matters pertaining to International Co-operation
10. Work allocation in case of Officers at the level of JS and above
11. Appointments in case of Officers at the level of DS and above in the Ministry
12. All appointments at the level of Directors and above in the Board of PSUs / other Organizations under the purview of the Ministry
Any other matters as deemed necessary by the Ministry or as directed by the Hon'ble Minister
